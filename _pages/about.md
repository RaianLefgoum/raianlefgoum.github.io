Welcome to my webpage!

i'm an optimization and machine learning engineer at <a href="https://www.eurodecision.com">EURODECISION</a> and currently on a mission at <a href="https://www.banque-france.fr/fr">Banque de France</a>, working on an optimization engine for the settlement of financial transactions. 

I obtained my PhD in Applied mathematics in September 2025 at Cermics, Ecole nationale des ponts et chaussées under the supervision of <a href="http://cermics.enpc.fr/~jpc/">
Jean-Philippe Chancelier </a>. The topic of my thesis is the multistage stochastic optimization of hydrogen supply chains. You can find my thesis manuscript <a href="https://theses.fr/2025ENPC0035">here</a>.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I obtained an engineering degree in computer science from École Nationale Supérieure d’Informatique, Algiers (2020), followed by a Master’s degree (Master 2) in Applied Mathematics and computer science from Paris Dauphine-PSL (2021).
